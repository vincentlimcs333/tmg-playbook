Phase 2 · Step 5
Show Unit ROI Calculator
Establish projected revenue, investment required, and breakeven units. Go/No-Go rule: investment must be below projected units × RM 2,500 cost per sale (or below 4% × avg ticket × units if ticket size exceeds RM 120,000). Timing check: minimum 8 active weeks of selling window required after the show unit is ready — anything less is a No Go.
